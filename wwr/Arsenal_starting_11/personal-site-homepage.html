Arsenal's Best Starting 11

Formation Diagram

The Arsenal "Invincibles" of the 2003/2004 season are a legendary team in football history, achieving the remarkable feat of completing an entire Premier League season unbeaten—a record of 26 wins and 12 draws. Managed by Arsène Wenger and led by iconic players like Thierry Henry, Dennis Bergkamp, and Patrick Vieira, this team embodied excellence, resilience, and teamwork. I created this project to celebrate their unparalleled achievement, showcase their incredible lineup, and highlight the tactical brilliance and legacy they left in the sport. Their journey is a source of inspiration, and this project aims to honor their contribution to football history. .
